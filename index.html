About Andy

Welcome to my online portfolio! I'm a dedicated web developer based at the University of Houston, in the Division of Advancement and Alumni, where I specialize in developing and maintaining web applications that streamline complex and labor-intensive tasks.

My most recent project involves an application that restructures an Excel file containing 9,000+ donor receipt records and converts them into a readable HTML format. This application then automatically sends out these records as email receipts. To avoid overwhelming the SMTP server, it features a built-in function that strategically spreads out the sending of these emails. I developed this app to help my team and save them thousands of hours that would otherwise be spent sorting data and sending emails manually.

As a seasoned full-stack developer committed to solving big problems with smart code, I am currently enhancing my skills in JavaScript. I continue to pursue innovative ideas in both front-end and back-end frameworks—specifically React.js and Node.js—to deliver dynamic and interactive web experiences.

Explore my site to learn more about my projects and how I leverage technology to address practical challenges through innovative web solutions.
